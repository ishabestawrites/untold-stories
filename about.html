ABOUT

Devbhoomipedia is a digital home for the stories, faith, culture, and forgotten voices of Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand — the land known as Dev Bhoomi. This platform is not just a website. It is an emotion. It is an attempt to preserve what mountains whisper and rivers remember. From ancient temples and local deities to folklore, festivals, pahari traditions, and unheard village stories — Devbhoomipedia documents the soul of the Himalayas.
